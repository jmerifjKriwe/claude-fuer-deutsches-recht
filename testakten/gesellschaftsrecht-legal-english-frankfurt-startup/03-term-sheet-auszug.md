# 03 Term Sheet Auszug

**Project Comet Moth — Series A Financing**
**Company:** Kometenfalter Systems GmbH
**Governing Law:** German law
**Language:** English, no German translation to be prepared unless required for notarial or court purposes

## Investment

Northbridge Growth III SCS and co-investors shall subscribe for newly issued Series A Preferred Shares for an aggregate subscription amount of EUR 5,200,000. The pre-money valuation shall be EUR 12,000,000 on a fully diluted basis, including an option pool of 12 percent to be created prior to closing.

## Liquidation Preference

The Series A Preferred Shares shall carry a 1x non-participating liquidation preference. Upon any liquidation event, including any sale of all or substantially all shares or assets of the Company, the Series A Investors shall receive, prior to any distribution to holders of Common Shares, an amount equal to the original issue price. Thereafter, the remaining proceeds shall be distributed pro rata to all shareholders on an as-converted basis, provided that the Series A Investors shall receive the higher of their preference amount or the amount they would have received on an as-converted basis.

## Anti-Dilution

The Series A Preferred Shares shall benefit from customary broad-based weighted average anti-dilution protection. If the Company issues shares or share equivalents at a price below the Series A issue price, the conversion ratio shall be adjusted accordingly. The parties shall agree in the long-form documents whether bridge financings, employee incentive plans and strategic issuances are excluded.

## Founder Vesting

All Founder Shares shall be subject to a 48-month vesting schedule with a 12-month cliff commencing on the closing date. In case of a Bad Leaver event, unvested shares and, at the option of the Series A Majority, vested shares may be acquired at nominal value. In case of a Good Leaver event, unvested shares may be acquired at nominal value and vested shares at fair market value.

## Transfer Restrictions

No shareholder may transfer shares without prior written consent of the Series A Majority, except for permitted transfers. Transfers shall be subject to rights of first refusal, tag-along rights and drag-along obligations. A drag-along may be exercised if shareholders holding at least 60 percent of the shares and the Series A Majority approve a bona fide third-party sale.

## Governance

The Company shall establish an advisory board consisting of five members. The Series A Majority shall have the right to appoint one investor director. The Company shall not take any reserved matter without prior written approval of the investor director.

Reserved matters include:

- Annual budget and business plan.
- Debt exceeding EUR 150,000.
- Hiring or dismissal of C-level employees.
- Any change to the articles of association.
- Issuance of shares or share equivalents.
- Acquisition or disposal of material IP.
- Settlement of litigation exceeding EUR 75,000.

## Binding Effect

This Term Sheet is non-binding, except for confidentiality, exclusivity, costs, governing law and dispute resolution. Exclusivity shall expire six weeks after signing.
